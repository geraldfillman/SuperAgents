# Weekly Review Workflow

1. Review new companies added to the watchlist.
2. Update system-level TRL and milestone status.
3. Review budget concentration risk by agency and program.
4. Re-rank names by:
   - award velocity
   - milestone quality
   - financing durability
   - customer concentration
5. Identify systems that need manual analyst verification.
