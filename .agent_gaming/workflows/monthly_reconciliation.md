# Monthly Reconciliation Workflow

Run on the first day of each month to reconcile the full gaming coverage universe.

## Steps

1. Reconcile title statuses
   - Move titles between pre-production, alpha, beta, gold, launched, delayed, and canceled states
   - Preserve release-date history rather than overwriting it

2. Reconcile financing assumptions
   - Refresh runway estimates for all covered studios
   - Compare expected milestone receipts against observed disclosures

3. Review post-launch performance
   - Compare first-30-day concurrency against internal expectations
   - Flag titles with critic or engagement underperformance

4. Clean up stale or duplicate aliases
   - Preserve rebrands, working titles, and regional title variants

5. Refresh the target universe
   - Add newly listed small and mid-cap studios
   - Archive studios that no longer meet scope or no longer publish games
